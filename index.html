Highlight: Hiking the Inca Trail

One of my most unforgettable adventures was hiking the Inca Trail to Machu Picchu in Peru. Most often, tourists take a bus up from the remote town of Aguas Calientes to get to this Wonder of the World instead of hiking the 26-mile path. The journey took four days, winding through breathtaking Andean scenery, ancient ruins, and cloud forests.
